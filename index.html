OUR RECIPES

CHICKEN CURRY
$12.50

Ingredients:
500g chicken, cut into pieces, 1 cup plain yogurt, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 2 tbsp vegetable oil, 2 onions chopped, 2 tomatoes chopped, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 cup water, Salt to taste.

Instructions:
Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Marinate for 30 minutes.
Cook Curry: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
Combine: Add marinated chicken, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook for 10 minutes.
Simmer: Add water, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through. Serve hot with rice or naan.

ROASTED CHICKEN
$12

Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (3-4 pounds), 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper , 1 lemon, halved 4 garlic cloves, crushed, 1 bunch fresh thyme (optional), 1 bunch fresh rosemary (optional), 1 onion, quartered.

Instructions:
Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
Prepare Chicken: Rinse the chicken inside and out and pat dry with paper towels.
Combine:Rub the chicken with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stuff the cavity with lemon halves, garlic, and herbs. Place onion quarters around the chicken in the roasting pan.
Roast Chicken: Roast for about 1 hour and 20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
Rest and Serve: Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. Serve with your favorite side dishes.

CHICKEN WINGS
$11.50

Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken wings, Salt and pepper, to taste 1/2 cup honey, 1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1 teaspoon grated ginger, 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional), Chopped green onions, for garnish.

Instructions:
Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
Prepare Chicken Wings:Season the chicken wings with salt and pepper. Place them on a baking sheet.
Make Honey Glaze:In a small saucepan, combine honey, soy sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally (about 5-7 minutes).
Coat and bake: Pour half of the honey glaze over the chicken wings and toss them to coat evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes
Serve: Garnish with chopped green onions, and serve hot.

FRIED CHICKEN
$10.50

Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut into pieces, 2 cups buttermilk, 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp black pepper, 1 tbsp paprika, Vegetable oil, for frying.

Instructions: Marinate chicken pieces in buttermilk for 1 hour. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, coat evenly. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C). Fry chicken until golden brown, about 12-15 mins. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot. Enjoy crispy fried chicken!

FRESH JUICE
$3.30

Ingredients:
4-5 large oranges, Sugar (optional), Ice cubes (optional).

Instructions:
Wash and cut: Wash oranges and cut in half.
Juice: Squeeze the oranges using a juicer.
Strain (optional): Strain the juice to remove pulp if desired.
Sweeten (optional): Add sugar to taste and stir.
Serve:: Pour over ice cubes and enjoy immediately.

NOTE: Fruits can change as per your request

COCKTAIL
$5.20

Ingredients:
2 cups fresh watermelon, cubed and deseeded, 2 ounces vodka (optional for a non-alcoholic version), 1 tablespoon lime juice, 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, adjust to taste), Ice cubes, Fresh mint leaves for garnish, Lime slices for garnish.

Instructions:
Blend: Blend watermelon cubes and strain the juice.
Mix : In a shaker, combine watermelon juice, vodka (if using), lime juice, and sugar; shake well.
Pour Cocktail: Pour the watermelon mix into the glasses.
Garnish and Serve: Garnish with mint leaves and a lemon slice; serve immediately.

MOJITO
$4.30

Ingredients:
lemon, cut into wedges, 10-12 fresh mint leaves, 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste), 1/2 cup soda water (or sparkling water), 1/2 cup club soda, Ice cubes, 1-2 ounces white rum (optional for a non-alcoholic version), Extra mint leaves and lemon slices for garnish.

Instructions:
Muddle: Mash lemon wedges, mint leaves, and sugar in a glass.
Add Ice: Fill the glass with ice cubes.
Add Liquids: Pour in white rum (optional), soda water, and club soda; stir.
Garnish and Serve: Garnish with mint leaves and a lemon slice; serve immediately.

MILKSHAKES
$5.50

Ingredients:
2 cups vanilla ice cream, 1 cup milk, 1/4 cup chocolate syrup, Whipped cream (for topping), Chocolate shavings or sprinkles (optional, for garnish).

Instructions:
Blend: In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup.
Mix : Blend until smooth and creamy.Pour the milkshake into glasses.
Garnish and Serve: Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings or sprinkles, if desired.serve immediately.
